By The Numbers: The Health Center

By James Hovet

October 2, 2017

Of the 150 students surveyed, the majority only ever go to the Health Center when sick, regardless of form.

Of those 97 respondents who took perscription medication, 31 percent are required to keep some or all of their medication in the Health Center, down from 45 percent last year.

53 percent of students think that the Health Center is easy to access, with those who visit daily 20 percent less likely to say that it is easy to access.
