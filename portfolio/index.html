Merrily We Roll Along: The Dolphin Show at Northwestern University, Winter Quarter 2021-2022

The Dolphin Show is the largest student-produced musical in the country; a combined team of over 160 students come together every year to produce a show in Northwestern University's 1000-seat Cahn Auditorium. Director: Nora Geffen ('22); Co-LD: Henry Muller ('22); Set Design: Zoe Snead ('23); Costume Design: Emily Munster ('22)

ReFusionShaka: Northwestern University, Fall Quarter, 2021-2022

ReFusionShaka is Northwestern's largest student dance performance of the year. Three dance groups join together to perform three sold-out shows in Northwestern's 1000-seat Cahn Auditorium. Lighting design duties split with Henry Muller ('22)

Mee-Ow Comedy: Arts Alliance at Northwestern University, Winter Quarter 2019-20

Mee-Ow Comedy's unique mix of improv, music, and sketch comedy have made it a Northwestern insitution with an enduring legacy. Since its first show in 1974, four of its alumni have gone on to write for or star on SNL. Set Design: Sammy Koolik ('22)
