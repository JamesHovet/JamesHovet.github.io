Merrily We Roll Along: The Dolphin Show at Northwestern University, Winter Quarter 2021-2022

The Dolphin Show is the largest student-produced musical in the country; a combined team of over 160 students come together every year to produce a show in Northwestern University's 1000-seat Cahn Auditorium. Spring Awakening: Northwestern University, Spring Quarter, 2021-2022

My lighting for Spring Awakening had two goals. The first was to provide the scale of rock-concert-style lighting that the catharsis of Totally Fucked, the explosive longing of The Bitch of Living, and the fury of Don't Do Sadness all deserved. The second was to take Shanley Pavilion, the 2000 square foot storage-shed-turned-student-performance-venue, and turn it into a theater space capable of creating the internal "song worlds" of these characters as they sing about self-discovery, trauma, lonliness, and a brigher future. Director: Sunnie Eraso ('23); Set Design: Zoe Snead ('23); Costume Design: Annalise Biesterfeld ('23)

Mee-Ow Comedy at Northwestern University, Winter Quarter 2021-22

Mee-Ow Comedy's unique mix of improv, music, and sketch comedy have made it a Northwestern insitution with an enduring legacy. Since its first show in 1974, four of its alumni have gone on to write for or star on SNL. This iteration of the Mee Ow Show's theme was Mee-Ow You See Me and was put up in Shanley Pavilion. Set Design: Sammy Koolik ('22)
